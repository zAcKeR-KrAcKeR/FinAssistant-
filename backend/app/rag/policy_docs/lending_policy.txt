FINSIGHT LENDING POLICY DOCUMENT
Version 3.2 | Effective: January 2024
Classification: Internal Use Only

1. LOAN ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

1.1 Minimum Requirements
All applicants must meet the following baseline criteria to be eligible for loan consideration:
- Minimum age: 21 years; Maximum age at loan maturity: 70 years
- Minimum annual income: INR 1,80,000 (Rupees One Lakh Eighty Thousand)
- Minimum CIBIL credit score: 600 (for standard products); 550 (for secured products with guarantor)
- Indian resident or NRI with Indian source of income
- Valid KYC documents: PAN, Aadhaar, address proof

1.2 Debt-to-Income Ratio (DTI) Limits
The maximum allowable DTI ratio for new loan approvals is:
- Home Loans: 45%
- Personal Loans: 40%
- Vehicle Loans: 42%
- Business Loans: 50% (with audited financials for 2+ years)
- Education Loans: 35% (post-disbursement income considered)
Applications with DTI exceeding these thresholds require Senior Underwriter approval.

1.3 Loan Amount Limits by Product
- Personal Loan: Minimum INR 50,000; Maximum INR 25,00,000
- Home Loan: Minimum INR 5,00,000; Maximum INR 5,00,00,000 (subject to LTV)
- Vehicle Loan: Minimum INR 1,00,000; Maximum INR 50,00,000
- Business Loan: Minimum INR 2,00,000; Maximum INR 2,00,00,000 (with financials)
- Education Loan: Minimum INR 50,000; Maximum INR 75,00,000

2. CREDIT SCORING TIERS

2.1 CIBIL Score Bands and Applicable Interest Rate Premiums
- Score 800-900 (Excellent): Base rate applicable; auto-approve if other criteria met
- Score 750-799 (Very Good): Base rate + 0.25%; standard processing
- Score 700-749 (Good): Base rate + 0.75%; standard processing
- Score 650-699 (Fair): Base rate + 1.50%; additional income verification required
- Score 600-649 (Marginal): Base rate + 2.50%; guarantor or collateral required
- Score 550-599 (Poor): Base rate + 4.00%; secured products only; Senior UW approval
- Score below 550: Decline; refer to credit rehabilitation programme

3. LOAN-TO-VALUE (LTV) RATIOS
- Residential property (self-occupied): Maximum 80% LTV
- Residential property (investment): Maximum 70% LTV
- Commercial property: Maximum 65% LTV
- Vehicle (new): Maximum 90% LTV
- Vehicle (used, <3 years): Maximum 75% LTV
- Vehicle (used, 3-7 years): Maximum 60% LTV

4. DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

4.1 Salaried Applicants
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statements
- Form 16 / ITR for last 2 years
- Employer letter (if income >INR 15L per annum)

4.2 Self-Employed / Business Owners
- Last 2 years ITR with computation
- Last 2 years audited financials (if turnover >INR 40L)
- Last 12 months bank statements (all accounts)
- Business registration certificate
- GST registration (if applicable)

5. PRODUCT-SPECIFIC POLICIES

5.1 Personal Loans
- Minimum 6 months employment continuity with current employer (salaried)
- No more than 2 active unsecured loans at time of application
- Minimum 2 years in business (self-employed)
- Maximum loan tenure: 60 months

5.2 Home Loans
- Property must be legally clear; title search mandatory
- Technical valuation mandatory; bank-approved valuer list applies
- MODT / equitable mortgage required for all home loans
- Maximum tenure: 30 years (subject to retirement age)

5.3 Business Loans
- Business must be operational for minimum 3 years
- Last 2 years should be profit-making
- All directors/partners to be co-applicants
- Working capital and term loan requirements to be assessed separately

6. APPROVAL AUTHORITY MATRIX

| Loan Amount        | Approval Authority          |
|-------------------|-----------------------------|
| Up to INR 5L      | Junior Underwriter          |
| INR 5L – 25L      | Senior Underwriter          |
| INR 25L – 100L    | Credit Manager              |
| INR 100L – 500L   | Chief Credit Officer        |
| Above INR 500L    | Credit Committee (quorum)   |

7. EXCEPTIONS AND DEVIATIONS

7.1 Deviation Policy
Any deviation from standard policy requires documented justification and approval one level higher than normal authority.
Maximum 2 deviations permitted per file; no deviations on KYC non-compliance.

7.2 Geographic Restrictions
Loans in flood-prone, seismic zone IV/V, or areas under legal dispute require additional due diligence and 10% additional margin on LTV.

8. POLICY REVIEW
